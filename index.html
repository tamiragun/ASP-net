About

Have you ever wondered what the right dosage is of the medicine you're taking, or what its side effects are? The meds come with instructions, but those are often only written in some South African languages.

RainbowMeds helps you understand exactly what you're taking, by translating critical pharmaceutical information into your home language.

You don't need an account, and you don't need to pay a cent. Just send a WhatsApp, right from the pharmacy or doctor's rooms!
